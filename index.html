About Us

Karrington Private Homecare was founded by a former carer with 31years of hands-on experience in most types of care including Dementia, Alzheimers, End of Life, Disability, Psychotic, Bipolar and Elderly care.

We take pride in the fact that our team is made up of former carers who have first-hand knowledge of the challenges faced by families who have a loved one that needs care or support.

Our rigorous recruitment process ensures we select carers who share our vision and values on respect, dignity, safe guarding and confidentiality to our clients, always.

Our goal is to grow our company’s reputation by connecting highly knowledgeable, kind and capable live In carers to private families across the UK,thus making Karrington Private Homecare a go to Agency for Live in care services.

We pride ourselves on offering our clients and their families dignity and peace of mind with a combination of high standards, kindness, understanding and confidentiality at all times.

From our base in Southgate, London, we operate all over the UK and would love to hear from you about your care needs for you or your loved one.
